Unnamed Game - Help

How to play

  • You control a fleet of tiny ships
    • Left click to select
    • Right click to send to a position or orbit
  • Ships are automatically produced at your home moon
  • Control your economy using the spending slider
    • 100% produces ships as fast as possible
    • 0% produces no ships, but invests to increase your income over time
  • Neutral ships orbit grey moons
    • They don't attack, but do fire back
    • You receive a bonus for each neutral ship you destroy
  • The enemy's base is a red moon
    • Enemy ships attack both neutrals & you
    • Orbit an enemy base that has no defenders and you will gradually conquer it
  • Your objective is to conquer the enemy moon before they conquer yours
